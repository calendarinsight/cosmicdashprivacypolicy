Cosmic Dash - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 6, 2025

Introduction

Cosmic Dash ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you play our mobile game.

Information We Collect

Gameplay Data: We collect anonymous gameplay statistics including scores, achievements, and game progress to provide game functionality and leaderboard features.

Device Information: We may collect anonymous device identifiers for analytics and to prevent fraud.

Leaderboard Names: If you choose to participate in leaderboards, we collect the display name you provide.

Third-Party Services

Our game uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Games: For achievements and leaderboards. Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: For managing in-app purchases. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Advertising

We display ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may use cookies and device identifiers to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Cosmic Dash is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data by contacting us. You can also reset your advertising identifier in your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.
